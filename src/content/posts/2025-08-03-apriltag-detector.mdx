I've been wanting to detect AprilTags using my Android phone for a while now. There's [an app](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=edu.umich.eecs.april.apriltag&hl=en), but it doesn't support the tag families I want.

Luckily someone had already done the hard work of convincing Emscripten to compile the AprilTag library to WASM (see [arenaxr/apriltag-js-standalone](https://github.com/arenaxr/apriltag-js-standalone)).

So without further ado: here's [AprilTag Detector](https://www.rossng.eu/apriltag-mobile/), a way to detect AprilTags using just your web browser.

Features:

- Tag families: `tag36h11`, `tag25h9`, `tag16h5`, `tagStandard41h12`, `tagStandard52h13`, `tagCircle21h7`, `tagCircle49h12`, `tagCustom48h12`
- Live detection using your device camera
- Record all tags seen during a period of time (useful for surveying an area)
- Upload an image from your device
